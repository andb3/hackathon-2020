Environmental Change

Read more

Environmental change is the way that the Earth is changing due to human influences. Climate change is a huge factor of environmental changes and is something that impacts each and every one of us. It is our obligation to take initiative and do what we can to ensure the health of our planet.

Pollution

There are several different types of pollution, including noise pollution, light pollution, sediment pollution, air pollution, littering, and water pollution. Each of these has detrimental effects on the environment, harming humans and animals alike.

Biodiversity Losses

Biodiversity is an important aspect of the food chain and the bio processes on Earth. When this delicate balance is disturbed we face catastrophic consequences.

Climate Change

Climate change is an environmental hazard that is caused by greenhouse gases such as methane and carbon dioxide. It is characterized as a pattern of changes in climates.

Urbanization

Urbanization includes forms of deforestation, and destruction of natural habitats in order to construct infrastructure and meet other human needs. Urbanization allows us to have homes at the expense of another animal.
